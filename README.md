ravelCheckinApp - Smart Travel & POS Ecosystem
I. Project Overview
TravelCheckinApp is a comprehensive, full-stack ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between tourists and service providers. The platform integrates POS (Point of Sale), PMS (Property Management System), and a Smart Check-in mechanism to streamline tourism operations, from hotel bookings to retail transactions.

II. Key Features
Smart Check-in & Ticket System: QR-based verification for users at tourist spots; owners can instantly confirm advance bookings and check in guests via mobile ticket scanning.

Integrated POS & Transaction Management: Handles both advance bookings and walk-in sales; includes advanced transaction classification for accurate financial reporting.

Voucher & Promotion Engine: Dedicated module for creating and validating user discount vouchers, enhancing customer engagement.

Commission & Revenue Tracking: Built-in logic to track commissions and revenue streams per service provider.

Emergency & AI Support: Integrated SOS emergency alerts and AI-driven data analytics for itinerary planning (part of "SmartData AI-Flow").
